travel
GETTING THERE

Mt. Woodson Castle is about a 45 minute drive from San Diego International Airport. For out of town guests, we recommend booking a rental car that is available for pick-up / drop-off at the airport. The venue has a parking lot and should have plenty of parking.



WHERE TO STAY

We recommend booking a hotel in Poway if you prefer to be closer to the venue. If you would like to explore San Diego, we suggest staying closer to the airport. Please feel free to book a stay where you are most comfortable.

We do have a room block in La Jolla, which is about 35 minutes from Mt. Woodson Castle. If you are interested, please contact us for more information!
